Chicken Alfredo

This is going to contain the recipe for chicken alfredo, Chicken Alfredo is a very popular dish made with pasta and many accessible/affordable ingredients

ingredients required:

  • One pound uncooked pasta
  • Two boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • One 16 oz jar Alfredo Sauce
  • One 10 oz packaged frozen mixed vegetables
  • One 4.5 oz can sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup of milk

Steps to make:

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to boil. Cook pasta at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
  2. While pasta is cooking, placed cubed cooked chicken, Alfredo sauce, frozen vegetables, mushrooms, and milk in a large saucepan over medium-low heat. Cook and stir until chicken is heated through and vegetables are tender.
  3. Serve warm Alfredo and chicken sauce over cooked noodles
