Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • One pound spaghetti
  • Two tablespoons olive
  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • Clove Garlic
  • 1/4 cup of dry white wine
  • Four large eggs beaten
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and Black pepper to taste
  • Two tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

How to cook carbonara:

  1. Bring pot to boil, Drain toss with 1 tablespoon of olive oil and set aside.
  2. Place diced bacon in large skillet save bacon fat
  3. Add one tablespoon of olive oil to bacon fat on skillet, add chopped onion and cook over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant. Add wine and cook one more minute.
  4. Return cooked bacon to skillet, add pasta. Add eggs and cook. Quickly add 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve warm with chopper parsley on top
